The Story

Built On A Simple Heist —

Take the best beef. The best buns. The kind of cheese that actually melts. And steal them away from the chains that hide them behind giant marketing budgets and average burgers.

Every burger is built around a 100g or 170g smashed patty on a Martin's potato bun — the bun the Americans use, the one with the soft pull and the perfect lift. Our wagyu's pressed fresh. Our chicken's hand-breaded. Our chips are crisp. And every order's made to order.

We named our burgers after outlaws because feeding Chester Hill burgers this good felt a little criminal. Eight hundred 5-star Uber Eats reviews later — guess we're not stopping.
